The 50/30/20 Rule

Consider dividing your income into three categories: 50% for Needs (bills, groceries), 30% for Wants (shopping, dining out), and 20% for Savings or Debt payoff. Check your Analytics page to see how your current spending aligns with this rule!

🛡️
Build an Emergency Fund

Aim to save at least 3 to 6 months' worth of living expenses. This acts as a financial safety net for unexpected events like medical emergencies or sudden appliance repairs. Start small by setting aside a fixed amount every payday.

⚡
Automate Your Savings

Don't rely on willpower alone to save. Set up automatic transfers from your checking account to your savings account on the day you get paid. If you never see the money in your primary account, you are much less likely to spend it.

📅
Review Subscriptions

Subscription fatigue is real. Take 10 minutes to review your monthly recurring bills. Cancel any streaming services, gym memberships, or software subscriptions that you haven't used in the last 60 days. Redirect that money into your savings!
